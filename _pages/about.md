I am a postdoc researcher of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), supervised by A/Prof. [Jin Zhang](https://faculty.sustech.edu.cn/zhangj4/). I obtained the Ph.D. Degree from the University of Technology, Sydney (UTS) through a joint SUSTech-UTS Ph.D. program, where I was supervised by A/Prof. [Jin Zhang](https://faculty.sustech.edu.cn/zhangj4/) and co-superviced by A/Prof. [Jing Jiang](https://profiles.uts.edu.au/Jing.Jiang/about). I received my B.E degree in computer science and engineering from SUSTech.

My current research focuses on volumetric video streaming. My previous research interests include video analytics in heterogeneous CPU-GPU systems and game theory in crowdsourcing and blockchain. I am **open to collaboration related to volumetric video streaming, immersive multimedia, or 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS)**.
